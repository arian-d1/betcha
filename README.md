# Betcha!

Whether you're watching the roulette wheel spin at the Casino or praying for the Lakers on Polymarket, you're playing
someone else's game!​

But now, with Betcha!™, you can be the player and the dealer.​

Betcha! is a one on one, choose your own wager, open market betting platform.​

Bet on your terms! Play your own game! Control the stakes!​

Want to show off your gaming prowess? Challenge the market to a 1v1 in your favorite game for a sweet cash prize.​

Think you're always right? Make a bet on Betcha! and see what poor fool may object your claim.​

Wanna make it rain from the ignorance of others? Check the market for delusion wagers and rake in the cash.​

Betcha! is betting without a house; just two people putting money where their mouth is.
